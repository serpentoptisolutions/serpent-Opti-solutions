What We Offer

A complete range of field services to keep HVAC, fire, plumbing and plant control systems safe, efficient and reliable.

Installation & Startup Support

On-site assistance during installation and startup – checking orientation, wiring, rotation, setpoints and basic readiness before systems are handed over.

Testing & Commissioning

Functional tests, calibration checks and control validation to confirm that equipment, panels and drives work together as intended.

Preventive & Corrective Maintenance

Planned checks and targeted corrective actions that help improve reliability and reduce unplanned shutdowns or repeat faults.

Fire System Services

Support for fire pumps, hydrant lines and sprinkler-related pumping systems, including sequence checks, alarms and performance tests.

HVAC & Hydronic Optimization

Performance tuning for hydronic circuits – flow balancing, differential pressure review and energy-focused adjustments to improve overall system behaviour.

Plant Control & Automation Support

Assistance with VFD setup, controller configuration, logic testing and coordination between pumps, panels and plant control systems.

Troubleshooting & Field Diagnostics

On-site technical investigation to reproduce issues, identify root causes and guide the corrective actions needed to stabilize operation.

Reporting & Recommendations

Clear, structured reports that summarize tests carried out, observations, and practical improvement steps for the project team.

How We Work on Site

A simple, four-step flow that keeps everyone – consultant, contractor and client – on the same page.

01

Discuss & Scope

Understand system type, issues, drawings and expectations before visiting the site.

02

On-site Assessment

Review panels, drives and system operation under relevant conditions and modes.

03

Settings & Tests

Apply agreed changes, test sequences and record how the system behaves.

04

Report & Handover

Provide a concise report with findings, changes made and further recommendations.
